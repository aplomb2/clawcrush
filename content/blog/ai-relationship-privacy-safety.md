## How ClawCrush Privacy Works

### Isolated Instances

Most AI companion apps run on shared infrastructure. When you chat with a Replika or Character.AI character, your messages pass through shared servers and may influence shared models.

ClawCrush is architecturally different. Each user gets a **completely independent AI agent** with:
- Its own memory files
- Its own conversation history
- Its own personality configuration
- No connection to other users' agents

Think of it as having a private office for your AI companion, not a shared cubicle.

### Zero Data Sharing

Your conversations are never:
- Used to train AI models
- Shared with other users
- Sold to advertisers
- Accessible by ClawCrush staff (without your permission)

### Telegram Encryption

ClawCrush operates through Telegram, which provides:
- Client-server encryption for all messages
- Optional Secret Chats with end-to-end encryption
- No message storage on servers after delivery
