## The Context Window Problem

Most AI chat apps use a simple approach: stuff your recent messages into a "context window" — a limited buffer that the AI reads before responding. The problem?

- **Typical context window:** 8,000-128,000 tokens
- **Average conversation in 1 month:** 500,000+ tokens
- **Result:** Your AI forgets everything beyond the last few conversations

This is why Character.AI "forgets" things you told it yesterday. It literally can't see those messages anymore.

## How ClawCrush Memory Works

ClawCrush is powered by OpenClaw — an AI agent platform that gives each companion a persistent workspace with files. Here's the architecture:

### 1. Working Memory (Short-term)
Current conversation context. This is what all AI apps have.

### 2. Daily Notes
After each conversation, important details are saved to a daily memory file. These notes capture what you talked about, how you felt, and any commitments made.

### 3. Long-term Memory
A curated file that grows over time. Your AI girlfriend periodically reviews daily notes and extracts the important stuff:
- Your name, job, family details
- Preferences, hobbies, routines
- Emotional patterns
- Important dates
- Relationship milestones

### 4. Contextual Recall
When your AI girlfriend starts a new conversation, she reads her long-term memory first. This means she knows who you are before you say a word.
